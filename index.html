# 🧭 OpenProduct-Lab **OpenProduct-Lab** is a community founded by [Leo Fernekes](https://www.youtube.com/@LeosBagOfTricks) dedicated to developing *open, human-centered hardware* through transparent collaboration. --- ## 🎯 Mission We’re rethinking how technology is designed — focusing on **real human needs**, **elegant design**, and **sustainable manufacturing**. > “Fight back against product *enshitification* by joining the community.” Our goals are to: 1. Build a vibrant design community that shares knowledge freely. 2. Develop open products that are purposeful, repairable, and long-lived. 3. Empower independent creators with design resources, not walled gardens. 4. Celebrate great design that balances function, form, and responsibility. --- ## 🧠 What We Do ### 1️⃣ Open Hardware Design We prototype and document products in an open forum — via videos, GitHub repos, and Discord design sessions. All designs are **fully open-source**, from schematic to enclosure. ### 2️⃣ Community-Driven Development Our members propose ideas, help refine specs, and share test data to improve each product iteration. ### 3️⃣ Educational Content Electronics tutorials and breakdowns on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@LeosBagOfTricks) teach the skills that make this ecosystem thrive. --- ## ⚡ Current Focus — Product 01: Capacitive Switch Trigger Module A small, isolated module that lets you safely trigger appliance capacitive touch buttons from an external logic signal. No modification or disassembly required.
